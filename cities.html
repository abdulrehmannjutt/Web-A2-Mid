My Favourite Cities




New York

New York City comprises 5 boroughs sitting where the Hudson River meets the Atlantic Ocean.
Its iconic sites include skyscrapers such as the Empire State Building and sprawling Central Park.
Broadway theater is staged in neon-lit Times Square.

Dubai

Dubai is a city and emirate in the United Arab Emirates known for luxury shopping,
ultramodern architecture and a lively nightlife scene.

Paris

Paris, France's capital, is a major European city and a global center for art, fashion,
gastronomy and culture. Its 19th-century cityscape is crisscrossed by
wide boulevards and the River Seine.
